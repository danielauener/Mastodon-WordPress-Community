WordPress Community on Mastodon

Follow the WordPress community on Mastodon

Follow and get in touch with the WordPress Community on Mastodon? Here we list all accounts, where the account owner has at least one communtiy badge on their WordPress.org profile page (example https://profiles.wordpress.org/wordpress/).

Just decide if you want to follow all or only some of the accounts listed below. You are provided with a .csv-file, which you can upload in Mastodon in order to follow all accounts included. Here you can find a short instruction, on how to do this.

Futher information on how to be added or removed can be found at the bottom of this page.

Clicking on a keyword allows you to filter the list; clicking the activated keyword again will undo the filter.

This list is curated by the Team of the Mastodon instance wptoots.social (currently 1 person :). If it saved you a lot of time, consider buying them a coffee:

Accounts by handle, name, and profile link (most recent first)

Full names are only included if they are shown in the profile or on explicit demand. You want to be on the list, too? Please contact me under @danielauener@wptoots.social. You may use the following template: 

Please add me to the WordPress Community Mastodon List
@handle@server, name, https://server/@handle, wordpress.org profile link, language(s) example: en de
#wordpress #communitylist

If you want to be removed from the list, you can contact in the same way as above.
